Why BizaroHQ

Everything your workshop needs,
in one place

Stop juggling paper registers, WhatsApp messages and Excel sheets. BizaroHQ brings your entire operation online — instantly.

Digital Job Cards

Create, assign and track job cards in seconds. Attach photos, technician notes and parts lists — fully paperless.

Auto Invoicing

Generate branded GST invoices automatically when a job is done. Share via WhatsApp or email in one tap.

Inventory & Parts

Track spare parts stock in real-time. Get low-stock alerts and auto-deduct parts when added to job cards.

Customer Records

Complete vehicle and service history for every customer. Send service reminders to drive repeat business.

Analytics & Reports

Daily, monthly and custom reports on revenue, jobs, technician performance and parts usage.

Multi-Staff Access

Role-based access for owners and staff. Owners see everything; staff see only their assigned jobs.
