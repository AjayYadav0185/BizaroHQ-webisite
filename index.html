Why BizaroHQ

Everything your workshop needs,
in one place

Stop juggling paper registers, WhatsApp messages, and Excel sheets. BizaroHQ brings your entire operation online — instantly.

Digital Job Cards

Create, assign, and track job cards in seconds. Attach vehicle photos, technician notes, and part lists — all paperless.

Auto Invoicing

Generate branded GST invoices automatically when a job is completed. Share via WhatsApp or email with one click.

Inventory & Parts

Track spare parts stock in real-time. Get low-stock alerts and auto-deduct parts when they are added to job cards.

Customer Records

Complete vehicle and service history for every customer. Send service reminders automatically to drive repeat business.

Analytics & Reports

Daily, monthly, and custom reports on revenue, jobs, technician performance, and parts usage — always up to date.

Multi-Staff Access

Role-based access for owners and staff. Owners see everything; staff see only their jobs — no data leaks, full control.
